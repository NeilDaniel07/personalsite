My Skills.

Percussion

After drumming for three years, I joined the school band at Lees Corner Elementary and took up percussion. I'm now in the Symphonic Band at Franklin Middle School.

Swimming

I've been in the pool since I was six months old. At six, I joined the community swim team and was selected for exclusive meets. Now, I swim in the Senior Silver division with the Potomac Marlins Swim Club.

Volunteering - JPMF Splash

Drumming

Since 2015, I have been drumming and performing with others at the community level. I drum daily and have performed twice.

Electronics & Circuits
